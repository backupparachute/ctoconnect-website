## What We Collect

When you sign up for CTOConnect, we collect:

- **Account information:** Your name and email address, provided during registration.
- **Billing information:** Payment details processed securely by our billing provider (see Third-Party Services below). We do not store your credit card number directly.
- **Usage data:** Anonymous, aggregated analytics about how you use our site (pages visited, general location by country). We do not track you across other websites.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and manage your CTOConnect membership
- Send you session reminders and membership-related communications
- Improve our content and services based on aggregate usage patterns

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate CTOConnect:

- **[Outseta](https://www.outseta.com/):** Handles authentication, membership management, and billing. Outseta processes your account and payment information. See [Outseta's Privacy Policy](https://www.outseta.com/privacy).
- **[Fathom Analytics](https://usefathom.com/):** Provides privacy-focused, cookieless website analytics. Fathom does not collect personal data or track you across sites. See [Fathom's Privacy Policy](https://usefathom.com/privacy).
- **[GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/):** Hosts our website. See [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

## Cookies

CTOConnect does not set tracking cookies. Outseta may use essential cookies to maintain your login session.

## Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your membership is active. If you cancel, your data is retained per Outseta's data retention policy. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Cancel your membership at any time

## Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at [kyle@ctoconnect.io](mailto:kyle@ctoconnect.io).
